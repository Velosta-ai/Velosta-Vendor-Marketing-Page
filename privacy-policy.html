01 Who We Are

Velosta is the team behind the Velosta Vendor app. For vendor (shop-owner) account data, we act as a Data Fiduciary as defined under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). We do not act as a processor for end-customer personal data — customer details that vendors save in the app stay on the vendor's device and are not uploaded to Velosta's servers.

02 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

(a) Vendor account information

  • Owner name, mobile number, email address, password (stored hashed)
  • Shop name, location, GSTIN (if applicable), business category
  • Staff names, mobile numbers and access role assigned by you

(b) Vehicle & booking data

  • Vehicle make, model, registration number, photos, rental rates
  • Booking start/end time, payments collected, deposit, fines, return condition
  • Before/after inspection photos uploaded by you

(c) Customer information you save on your device

Velosta does not collect or receive the details of your renting customers. Any customer information you capture in the app — including name, mobile number, photo, or any ID document the customer voluntarily shares — is stored only on your own phone. It is not uploaded to Velosta's servers and Velosta has no access to it.

We do not collect Aadhaar. Velosta does not ask for, receive, or store Aadhaar numbers or Aadhaar documents — from vendors or from their customers. The app does not integrate with the Aadhaar authentication or e-KYC systems. If you choose to record any customer ID (such as a driving licence) for your own shop's records, that record stays on your device only; you are responsible for obtaining the customer's consent and keeping it secure.

(d) Device, log & technical data

  • Device model, OS version, app version, language preference
  • IP address, approximate location (city-level), session timestamps
  • Crash logs and diagnostic events to keep the app stable

(e) Device permissions we request

The app requests the following Android runtime permissions. Where a permission is optional, you may decline without losing core functionality:

PermissionPurposeRequired?

Read/Write External StorageSelect vehicle and document images from your photo gallery; save generated receipts and booking PDFs to your deviceOptional

(f) Payments & subscription

  • Subscription plan, billing email, GSTIN for invoices, transaction reference IDs
  • We do not store full card numbers, CVVs or UPI PINs — these are handled by RBI-licensed payment gateways
  • Where required, we may collect KYC documents (e.g. PAN, GSTIN) from you for invoicing and taxation under the GST regime

03 How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above to:

  1. Provide, operate and improve the Service — including managing vehicles, bookings, payments, reports and messaging (customer records stay on your device);
  2. Authenticate you and prevent unauthorised access to your account;
  3. Send transactional communications (booking confirmations, return reminders, invoices, OTPs);
  4. Power safety features such as the Global Block Alert — where vendors can flag customers for misconduct (theft, damage, non-payment) so other genuine vendors are warned;
  5. Comply with applicable Indian laws, including responding to lawful orders from courts, police or government authorities;
  6. Communicate product updates, feature releases and (with your consent) marketing offers — you may opt out anytime;
  7. Detect, investigate and prevent fraud, abuse, security incidents and policy violations.

04 Legal Basis for Processing

Under the DPDP Act, we process personal data only on lawful grounds, namely:

  • Consent — given by you at or before the point of collection. You can withdraw consent at any time by writing to support@velosta.com; withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of prior processing;
  • Performance of contract — processing necessary to deliver the Service you have signed up for;
  • Legitimate uses as set out under Section 7 of the DPDP Act, including compliance with judgments and court orders, responding to emergencies, and fulfilling statutory obligations where applicable.

05 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers (Data Processors): cloud hosting (e.g. AWS Mumbai region, Google Cloud Mumbai), SMS and WhatsApp delivery partners, payment gateways and analytics tools — all bound by contractual data protection obligations;
  • Within the vendor community (Global Block Alert): if you — as a vendor — choose to raise a Global Block Alert against a customer for genuine misconduct (theft, damage, non-payment), only the customer's mobile number and your stated reason are shared with other Velosta vendors during a future booking attempt. No other customer detail (name, photo, ID, address) ever leaves your device. You are responsible for raising flags in good faith;
  • Legal & regulatory: in response to lawful requests from Indian government authorities, courts, the Data Protection Board of India, or where required to protect rights, life, property or safety;
  • Corporate transactions: in the event of a merger, acquisition or transfer of business, with notice to you as required by law.

06 Communications

The Service uses WhatsApp Business APIs and SMS to send transactional messages (booking confirmations, return reminders, payment receipts) to your customer's mobile number at the moment you trigger the message from your phone. The number is used only to dispatch that message — we do not retain the customer's contact list. By using this feature, you confirm that:

  • You have obtained the customer's prior consent to receive messages from your shop in connection with their booking;
  • You comply with WhatsApp's Business Messaging Policy and the Telecom Commercial Communications Customer Preference Regulations (TRAI TCCCPR), 2018, where applicable;
  • You will honour any opt-out request received from a customer.

08 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security practices and procedures as required under the SPDI Rules and aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 controls, including:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest for sensitive fields;
  • Customer details and any ID images you save never leave your phone — they are held in the app's local storage on your device, not on Velosta's servers;
  • Role-based access control — only a small number of authorised personnel may access production systems;
  • Daily encrypted backups with point-in-time recovery;
  • Periodic VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing) and code reviews;
  • Mandatory device PIN or biometric lock advised for all vendor users.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential.

09 Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you (the "Data Principal") have the following rights:

  • Right to access — obtain a summary of personal data we hold about you and the processing activities undertaken;
  • Right to correction & erasure — request correction of inaccurate data and erasure of personal data no longer needed;
  • Right of grievance redressal — raise complaints with us at support@velosta.com; we aim to acknowledge within 48 hours and resolve promptly;
  • Right to nominate — nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity;
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent for processing at any time, with the same ease as it was given.

To exercise any of these rights, please write to support@velosta.com. We will respond within the timelines prescribed by the DPDP Act and rules.

10 Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by vendors who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data of children (defined under the DPDP Act as individuals below 18 years). If you believe a child's data has been uploaded to the Service, please notify us at support@velosta.com and we will delete it promptly.

11 Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses essential cookies for session management. With your consent, we may also use first-party and third-party analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics) to understand aggregate usage. You can disable non-essential cookies via your browser settings.

12 Data Storage & Transfers

We primarily store and process your personal data on servers located in India. Where data is transferred outside India (for example, to a service provider's data centre), such transfer will be made only to jurisdictions that are not restricted by the Central Government under Section 16 of the DPDP Act, and subject to appropriate contractual safeguards.

13 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected Data Principals in the manner and within the timelines prescribed under the DPDP Act and rules. We will also coordinate with the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In) as required under the IT Act, 2000 and CERT-In Directions, 2022.

14 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, in technology, in legal requirements or in the Service. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. For material changes, we will provide notice through the app or by email. Continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
