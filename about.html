Our Story

Built after watching shop owners juggle three notebooks at once

Velosta started with a simple observation. Walk into any bike or car rental shop in India — Goa, Manali, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pondicherry — and you'll see the same scene. One notebook for bookings. Another for payments. A third for customer IDs. Photos of Aadhaar on the owner's personal WhatsApp. Calculations on the back of a receipt.

Most software built for "rental businesses" is made for big global fleets — complicated dashboards, English-only support, monthly fees that make no sense for a shop with 8 bikes and 2 cars. So owners stuck with the notebooks. And lost track. And lost money.

We built Velosta Vendor for them. Phone-first. Made for India. Quick to learn — if you can use WhatsApp, you can use Velosta. Honest pricing in rupees. Real human support in Hindi and English over WhatsApp. No 30-tab dashboards. No jargon. Just the things a rental shop actually needs to run cleanly and grow.

Today, shop owners across 50+ Indian cities use Velosta Vendor to manage their vehicles, take bookings, send WhatsApp receipts, verify customer IDs and track their earnings — without a computer, without a manager, without the chaos.
